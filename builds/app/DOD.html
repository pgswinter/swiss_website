    Engineering Services

    NVH & Design Service

    From a single noise issue resolution to completeassembly analysis, we can help you from the concept phase to the series production in exterior and interior automotive parts.

    Beneficts

    1. Costs: Squeak and rattle noises arise very late in Project phases, and it has a very negative impact in Project costs. We anticipate all the noise risks in concept and development phase.
    2. Simplified manufacturing process: By implementing anti-noise measures in the design phase you can save extra manufacturing stations for felts or greases
    3. Less resources needed: The anticipation to the noise risks in early phases of the project prevents the use of your engineering resources and effort at pre-series phase
    4. No warranty claims: by performing an NVH study with us you are reducing to the minimum the risk of noise
    5. Company reputation improved: Whether your company is a car maker or an automotive supplier, your customers take the NVH performance as an added value of the product.
