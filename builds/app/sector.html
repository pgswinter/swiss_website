Aerospace and Defense

After a decade of steady growth, the Aerospace & Defence industry faces major challenges. The increasing globalization process, along with complex government regulations, creates more pressure than ever to improve productivity and increase transparency.

To meet with the growing international demand, and more particularly of China and the South Asian countries, companies have to modernise their fleet and rethink their processes in order to offer customised aircrafts with speed and flexibility. This also calls for a better control of the cost structure, but not to the detriment of environmental concerns and regulations. The defence firms, which face in addition a declining budget environment in many occidental countries, have to reinvent themselves to find efficient solutions and maintain their growth.

Amaris provides state-of-the-art services to the Aerospace & Defence industry. Our consultants operate at all stages of the value chain and in all functional and technical domains, through product engineering (electronics, mechanical, electrical, networks & protocols, embedded systems, mechatronic & controls) and process engineering (project management, quality assurance & control, supply chain & logistics, production management & optimization, RAM/RAMS analysis, certification & homologation).
